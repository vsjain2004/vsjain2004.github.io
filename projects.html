Processors for CprE 381 - Fall 2023

CprE 381 is a course on Computer Organization and Assembly level Programming focused on the basics of processor design and MIPS architecture. There were two major projects in the course during which three different processor designs were developed to fit a subset of the MIPS architecture. All designs were programmed in VHDL and were tested in Modelsim. The designs then went through a timing analysis in Quartus Prime based on a selected FPGA hardware. Learn more

ComS 309: CyHawk Clash - Fall 2023

ComS 309 is a course focused on learning about Android App Development using Java. Over the course of the semester, groups of 4 people develop an Android app. For the Forntend we used Android Studio, for the Backend we used SpringBoot and for our Database we used MySQL. Aside from basic Forntend and Backend code, we used advanced tools like Websockets as well as third-party APIs.

CprE 288 Project - Spring 2023

CprE 288 is a course focused on the basics of Embedded Systems and Embedded Systems programming. It involves writing scripts in C to control a Roomba. The Roombas have been adapted with a baseboard that connect to a WiFi antenna and a Texas Instruments board. For the final project, groups of three to four people work to create a situation that can be simulated using Roomba and then program it to do so. Learn more
